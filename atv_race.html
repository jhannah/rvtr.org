Sat Sep 28 2013 - ATV Race

We've confirmed with OMA Nationals that they will be holding a non-points ATV race at our park the day before the motorcycle points race.

Their website does not list this race because it is non-points and they don't want to confuse their points riders with this late addition to their season.

More details to come as the event draws closer.
