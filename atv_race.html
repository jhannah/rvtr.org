Sat Sep 28 2013 - ATV Race

We've confirmed with OMA Nationals that they will be holding a non-points ATV race at our park the day before the motorcycle points race.

The OMA website does not list this race because it is non-points and they don't want to confuse their points riders with this late addition to their season.

  • 11:00am ATV start (no practice)
  • Classes: Pro, A, B, plus 30, Utility, C
  • 100% + $100.00 Pro cash payout
  • 50% Trophy awards for all classes
  • No national media exposure, no points
  • Race will run 1h 45m and a lap to the leader
